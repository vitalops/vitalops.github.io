Developer Relations

On every release, agents generate changelogs, update affected docs, produce walkthrough videos, publish announcements, and monitor replies, all evaluated against an accuracy bar your team sets.

ML Research

Agents collect training data, run experiments, score results against your benchmark, identify failure modes, adjust the approach, and surface a progress report, in a continuous loop until a blocker requires you.

🧬

Clinical Research

Agents scan literature, draft and revise trial protocols against FDA and EMA checklists, monitor incoming data for safety signals, and trigger amendments, flagging only decisions that require a clinician.

📈

Investment Research

Agents update financial models when new information arrives, rewrite investment theses when assumptions break, and stress-test positions, every output held to the standard of an investment committee presentation.

📰

Editorial & Journalism

Agents monitor every beat around the clock, research and verify every claim against primary sources, and produce drafts evaluated against editorial standards, so editors see polished copy, not first drafts.

📋

Underwriting

Agents assess risk, price submissions, generate policy terms, and validate against appetite guidelines, producing a bindable quote without touching an underwriter's desk unless the risk falls outside defined parameters.

How it works

Simple to start. Built to last.

01

Tell us how your department runs

No forms to fill. No software to learn. We sit with your team and learn exactly how work moves through your department, the normal cases, the exceptions, and the things that always seem to fall through the cracks.

At the end of this, you have a clear picture of what's automatable and what genuinely needs a human. Your team helps define both.

"We just talked through what our team does every day. VitalOps turned that into something that actually runs."

✓Your team's knowledge stays with you
✓Every edge case is accounted for
✓You decide what agents are allowed to do
02

We build agents that know your work

We build a team of agents, each one responsible for a specific slice of your workflow. They connect to the tools you already use. They learn the standards your team follows. And before they touch anything real, they're tested against actual scenarios from your department.

You see exactly how each agent behaves, and sign off before anything goes live.

Works with what you already have
Connects to your existing tools, systems, and data, nothing needs to be rebuilt or replaced.
Tested before it matters
Every agent runs through realistic scenarios from your workflow, with no risk to your live operations.
You approve before go-live
Nothing is deployed until you've seen it work and said yes.
03

Agents start working. Your team stops chasing.

Agents run in the background, picking up tasks, handing off to each other, flagging anything that needs a person. Your team gets the outcomes without doing the work to get there.

Everything runs in a secure environment that you control. Your data doesn't move anywhere it isn't supposed to. If your industry requires it, agents can run entirely inside your own systems.

→

Multiple agents work in parallel, they don't wait for each other unless they have to.

→

When something is uncertain, they stop and ask, they don't guess.

→

Runs in your cloud, your data center, or ours, whichever your team requires.

04

You always know what's happening

Every action an agent takes is recorded, not as a summary, but in full detail. You can look back at any point and see exactly what happened, what decision was made, and why. This isn't a nice-to-have, it's what makes the whole thing trustworthy.

You also control exactly who on your team can see or change anything. Access is set by role, not by default, and you can update it at any time.

Full history, always
Nothing is ever deleted or changed after the fact. If you need to explain what happened, for a client, an audit, or your own team, it's all there.
Set limits on what agents can spend
You decide how much any agent or workflow is allowed to run before it pauses and checks with you first.
Roll back if needed
Every version of every agent is saved. If something changes and you want to go back, you can, without losing any history.
